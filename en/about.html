How It Works

Electric motors shift racks left and right, opening an aisle only where needed

1

Controller Operation

Select the desired aisle via the electric controller or smartphone app. Safety sensors activate automatically while the aisle opens.

2

Automatic Rack Movement

The electric motor smoothly drives the racks along rails (or floor plates). Near-silent operation with support for loads of several tens of tonnes.

3

Forklift Entry & Operation

Once the aisle is fully open, a forklift enters to load or unload. Closing the aisle afterwards restores maximum storage density.

Technology Types

Choose the right system for your site conditions

RAIL TYPE

Rail Type

Racks travel along floor-embedded rails, delivering superior straight-line accuracy. Ideal for large-scale and cold-storage warehouses requiring high load capacity.

  • ✓ Best for maximum load handling
  • ✓ Optimized for cold/frozen storage
  • ✓ High travel precision
NO-RAIL TYPE

No-Rail Type

Installs directly onto existing floors with no civil works required. Shorter installation time and easy relocation make it popular for leased or medium-sized warehouses.

  • ✓ No floor construction needed
  • ✓ Lower installation & relocation cost
  • ✓ Ideal for leased warehouses
